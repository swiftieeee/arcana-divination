ARCANA — custom card art
=========================
Drop your own images in this folder (images/cards/) using the EXACT
filenames below. Any card you don't provide simply keeps its built-in
generated illustration — you can replace one card, ten, or all 78.

Default format is .jpg. If your images are .png or .webp, open app.js
and change the CARD_IMAGE_EXT constant near the top to match.

Recommended: portrait orientation, roughly 3:5 aspect ratio (matches
a standard tarot card). Images are cropped to fit, not stretched, so
close to that ratio looks best.
